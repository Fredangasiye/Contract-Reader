Sample Insurance Contract

POLICY NUMBER: INS-2024-12345
COVERAGE AMOUNT: R50,000 per claim
DEDUCTIBLE: R2,500 applies to all claims

IMPORTANT TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

1. REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
   All incidents must be reported to the police within 24 hours of occurrence.
   Failure to report within this timeframe may result in claim denial.

2. COVERAGE LIMITS
   The maximum payable amount is R50,000 per claim.
   Annual aggregate limit: R200,000

3. EXCLUSIONS
   - Pre-existing conditions are not covered
   - Claims arising from illegal activities are excluded
   - Damage caused by intentional acts is not covered

4. PREMIUM PAYMENT
   Monthly premium: R450
   Premium must be paid by the 1st of each month
   Grace period: 7 days
   Policy will lapse if premium is not received within grace period

5. CLAIM PROCESS
   Submit claim within 30 days of incident
   Provide all required documentation
   Claims processed within 14 business days

This is a legally binding contract. Please read all terms carefully.
